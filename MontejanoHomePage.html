Our Mission

Mobile Repair Ambulance believes that mobile repair should be something that anyone can have done without having to take time out of your day to do so. Instead of having to deal with taking time out of your day to drive across town to have your phone repaired, you can leave the driving to us and have your phone repaired where ever you are located. Whether it's at your home, job, or your in-laws, Mobile Repair Ambulance has you covered. Se Habla Espanol!

How it Works

First, the user must book an appointment, where they will specify which time and location best suit their schedule to have their device repaired.

Secondly, a certified technician will come to your location and repair your device on-spot, in their mobile repair vehicle or any other preferred location.

And Finally, after the repair is complete, the technician will ensure that the phone is working correctly in front of the client. The client then will have the option to pay via credit card, PayPal, or cash.

Why Choose Us?

We'll Save You Time - You won't have to go out of your way to bring your phone to us, as our trained technicians will come to your preferred location.

Reliable & Fast - Our technicians will have your phone up and running within 30 minutes or less!

Quality Parts - We use only quality parts to ensure that our customers will leave satisfied every time. We are so confident that you'll think the same about our parts, or we guarantee you your money back!
